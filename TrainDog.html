How to Train Your Dog

Sit

Hold a treat above your dog's head and slowly move your hand further up. Your dog will follow the treat with his nose, which will automatically make him sit. Reward this by giving him the treat.

High Five

First, show your sweet friend that this trick is based on giving paw. Raise your hand in small steps. When you reach about the height of his collar, start to turn your hand upwards a little at a time. Continue to use the signal word "paw". Only when your hand is pointing vertically upwards. Do you use the new work mark. "High Five. Say the signal word as soon as your dog's paw toches your vertical hand
